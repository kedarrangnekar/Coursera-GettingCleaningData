1. subject
         Identifier of the subject of analysis. Range [1,30]
2. activity_name
         Name of the activity performed by the subject.
            Values in the variable  --------
              ( i. LAYING
               ii. SITTING
               iii. STANDING
               iv. WALKING
               v. WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
               vi. WALKING_UPSTAIRS )
